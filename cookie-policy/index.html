Cookie Policy

Cookies, small text files placed on your machine, are used on asliceofcambridge.com to provide anonymised tracking data to Google Analytics. This anonymised data is used to optimise and improve asliceofcambridge.com for users. On visiting asliceofcambridge.com you will be notified about the use of cookies on this site and will be given the option to provide your consent. However, if you would prefer to disable cookies you can do so in your browser. More information about how to do this can be found in the help section of your browser or here: http://www.aboutcookies.org
